Submitting Your Game

When you're ready for xelly.games users to play your game, you can upload it from your xelly.games profile. (If you've already uploaded your game but want to update it to a new version or fix, read how to do that here.)

Stepsâ€‹

  1. On your profile, click the pen icon () to the right of the tabs:

  1. Then click "Upload your game" and fill out the modal:

  • Remember to pick the appropriate aspect ratio for your game!
  • Optionally, you can provide a comment or instructions for your game (140 characters max).
  1. Now your game will be waiting moderation. Sit tight! It can take a couple hours or days for your game to be approved.

  1. Once approved, your game will be live! You'll see it marked as Live in your profile.
