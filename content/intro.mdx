Making a game for **[xelly.games](https://xelly.games)** is hella fun!

But, wait! What _is_ **[xelly.games](https://xelly.games)**?

Well, it's a **social network** of sorts &mdash; but instead of "tweets", videos, or
images, our users post **"micro games"**: i.e., simple, readily playable games that
our friends can interact with directly from a scrollable feed.
