Before uploading your game to xelly.games:

- [x] Have you [tested](./advanced/testing-your-game) your game using different mobile device widths?
- [x] Did you specify the [correct game type](./build-a-game/main-ts#metadata) in the `metadata` exported from your `main.ts` source?
- [x] Does your game have good [performance](./advanced/performance) and is it memory efficient?
- [x] Are you emitting a [`xelly:terminate`](./advanced/lifecycle#xellyterminate) event on your `Engine` (if needed)?
- [x] Are you starting your game play on the [`xelly:enter`](./advanced/lifecycle#xellyenter) event (if needed)?
- [x] Have you reviewed the xelly.games [terms of service](https://xelly.games/static/tos)?
- [x] Did you have fun making your game!?
