Review the [**submission checklist**](../checklist) before you submit any game.
:::

When you're ready for [**xelly.games**](https://xelly.games) users to play your game,
you can **upload it** from your xelly.games **profile**. (If you've already uploaded
your game but want to update it to a new version or fix, read how to do that
[here](./updating).)

## Steps

1. On your profile,
click the pen icon (<PencilLine size={16} color={"#3B82F6"}/>) to the right
of the tabs:

<div style={{
    display: "inline-block",
    border: "5px solid #FF69B4",
    borderRadius: "10px",
    padding: "10px",
    margin: "10px"
}}>
![Profile](./img/profile.png)
</div>

2. Then click **"Upload your game"** and fill out the modal:

[//]: # (![Upload]&#40;./img/upload.png&#41;)

<div style={{
    display: "inline-block",
    border: "5px solid #FF69B4",
    borderRadius: "10px",
    padding: "10px",
    margin: "10px"
}}>
![Uploaded](./img/uploaded.png)
</div>

* Remember to pick the appropriate [**aspect ratio**](./device-dimensions#aspect-ratio) for your game!
* Optionally, you can provide a **comment or instructions** for your game (140 characters max).

3. Now your game will be **waiting moderation**. Sit tight! It can take a couple
hours or days for your game to be approved.

<div style={{
    display: "inline-block",
    border: "5px solid #FF69B4",
    borderRadius: "10px",
    padding: "10px",
    margin: "10px"
}}>
![Waiting](./img/waiting.png)
</div>

4. Once approved, your game will be **live**! You'll see it marked as **Live**
in your profile.

<div style={{
    display: "inline-block",
    border: "5px solid #FF69B4",
    borderRadius: "10px",
    padding: "10px",
    margin: "10px"
}}>
![Approved](./img/approved.png)
</div>
