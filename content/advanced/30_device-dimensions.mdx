Various mobile devices and screen sizes are used to access
**[xelly.games](https://xelly.games)**.

It's a good idea to make your game responsive, allowing for a range of
widths, from **340px** to **412px**, roughly.

It is common to use `engine.drawWidth` and `engine.drawHeight` when
laying out the entities of your game.

## Aspect Ratio

When you build your game, you'll target a given **aspect ratio**, one of:

- `default` (4:3 ratio)
- `tall` (≈ 4:5.2, or 1.75x taller than `default`).

You can select the aspect ratio when you upload your game and when testing
using the ["utility" tool](https://xelly.games/utility).

`default` aspect ratio, the Poker Solitaire
game on the right uses the `tall` aspect ratio:
