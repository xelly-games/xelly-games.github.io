Various mobile devices and screen sizes are used to access
**[xelly.games](https://xelly.games)**.

It's a good idea to make your game responsive, allowing for a range of
game resolution widths &mdash; widths between **340px** to **412px** are good targets.

It's common, for example, to use `engine.drawWidth` and `engine.drawHeight`
when laying out the entities of your game.

## Aspect Ratio

When you build your game, you'll target one of two supported **aspect ratios**:

- `default` (4:3 ratio)
- `tall` (≈ 4:5.2 &mdash; or 1.75x taller than the `default` ratio).

You will select an aspect ratio when you upload your game, or when testing
using `npm run watch:live` or the xelly.games [utility tool](https://xelly.games/utility).

Here are some examples. The Golf Solitaire game below uses a
`default` aspect ratio, while the Tetris
game after it uses the `tall` aspect ratio:
